  • ABOUT US

  • This project was established in order to help people decide what their best form of transportation to Sacramento State University would be. The website provides a survey as well as information about different forms of transportations such as walking, bikes, scooters, buses, light rails, cars, and carpools. In a short survey, users will be able to determine what form of transportation best suites them. Whether someone is just viewing campus or getting to their classes, our website will be able to help users understand an optimal way of arriving to campus. As there are quite an array of possible transportations to campus, our primary goal is to educate users by using this survey. All project goals are in correlation with the University Transportation and Parking Services (UTAPS) department of Sacramento State University which is also the project sponsor. We would like to thank you for your involvement in this project.
